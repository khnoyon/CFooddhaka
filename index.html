The Begining

It was a dream come true for us when we opened our own seafood restaurant in the heart of Dhaka. We had always loved cooking and eating fresh fish and shellfish, and we wanted to share our passion with others. We named our restaurant CFood, after the saltiness of the sea and the spices they used in their dishes. We decorated the place with blue and white colors, wooden tables and chairs, and pictures of the ocean. We hired friendly and professional staffs, and they made sure to source their seafood from local and sustainable suppliers. We offered a variety of dishes, from grilled salmon and shrimp curry to lobster bisque and crab cakes. Our restaurant soon became popular among locals and tourists alike, who praised their quality, taste, and service. We are proud of our achievement, and we looked forward to welcoming more customers to our seafood paradise.
